A little bit about us ...

Compass Tutoring is an online tutoring platform for students in grades 1 to 10. In a tutoring session, students will be provided with quality education and all the best academic resources. We provide students with academic support and tutors with opportunities. We aim to make quality tutoring accessible around the country for very reasonable and affordable costs.
