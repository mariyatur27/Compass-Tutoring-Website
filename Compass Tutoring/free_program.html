Compass Tutoring is here to help Ukrainian students!

Our tutoring organization is offering free tutoring services to Ukrainian children affected by the war.

Explore Register for Tutoring

We Want to Help. Now.

Here at Compass Tutoring, we understand that Ukrainian people have to undergo a lot of suffering due to the war. In order to support the people of Ukraine, our team is launching a new free program for all of the Ukrainian children who are affected by the war. We are extending our free online tutoring services to all Ukrainian children from grades 1 to 10 in English, French, math and sciences.

Who is this tutoring program for?

Ukrainian Students Affected By War

This free program is dedicated to helping all ukrainian students affected by the war in any way. This includes children who are still inside the country and don't have access to stable education, as well as those who have fled the country.

Ukrainian Students in Grades 1-10

This free program is focused on ukrainian students in grades 1 to 10. The curriculum that will be taught will be a mixture of North-American and Ukrainian curriculums, provided that most of our tutors are located in North-America.

Ukrainian Students Around the World

A lot of Ukrainian students have fled the country as refugees, and were forced to stay in neighbouring countries, as well as other European and North-American countries. Therefore, we are making this program accessible globally.

Our Services

Subjects That We Tutor:

English

English lessons on writing, reading, comprehension and speaking from native English-speaking tutors.

French

French lessons on writing, reading, comprehension and speaking from French-speaking tutors and students.

Mathematics

Lessons on mathematics from North-American and Ukrainian grade 1 to 10 curriculums from experienced tutors.

Sciences

Lessons on biology, chemistry and physics, as well as elemetary school sciences from experienced tutors.

Tutoring Session Format:

Absolutely Free

This program is absolutely free. All the tutoring lessons for Ukrainian students affected by war will be at no cost.

One-on-One Tutoring

Once you register, you will be paired with a tutor who will provide you with one-on-one attention and support.

1-hour long lessons, personlised for you

All of our tutoring lessons last for 60 minutes. However, you can sign up for multiple lessons a week.

We accept students from all over the world

Given the current situation, we will be welcoming Ukrainian students affeced by the war from all over the world.

Students, Register Today!

Step 1: Register!

If you are a Ukrainian student affected by the war and you are interested in our free tutoring program, your first step will be to register for a tutoring session. We will view your application and contact you within a few days.

Step 2: Finding a Tutor

Once you register, our team will carefully review your application and pair you with an appropriate tutor who will be able to assist you with your needs. Once you are paired with a tutor, they will contact you and introduce themselves via email. Please note that this process can take anywhere from 1 to 3 days.

Step 3: Starting Your Session

Once your tutor contacts you, you will be ready to begin your tutoring session. Your lessons will occur online, using Zoom, Google Meet or Skype. Each lesson will be 1 hour long, and will focus on the subject that you selected in your registration. One tutoring session will last for 2 months. You can register for tutorig sessions as many times as you want.

You want to help the cause? Become a Tutor!

If you are someone who is looking for ways to support Ukrainians and is interested in helping us in promoting our cause, we invite you to submit a volunteer tutoring application. We welcome individuals from different education and work backgrounds, who have some tutoring experience and knowledge of the subjects that we offer. Our tutoring application for this program is always open and we are always looking for more volunteers!

Details:

  • You can be tutoring from anywhere in the world!
  • You must be at least 15 years of age. High-school students, university students and adults are all welcomed!
  • You must have access to a stable internet and a computer, as well as be able to use google applications (Google Docs, Google Slides, etc) or Microsoft (Powerpoints, Docx, Exel, etc).
  • You must have relevent knowledge of the subject that you are signing up to tutor.
  • Knowledge of Ukrainian language, and/or fluency in French and English is in high demand!
  • After you submit your application, we will schedule a brief interview with you. You will be emailed the instructions.
  • This is a volunteering position, tutors will not be paid.
