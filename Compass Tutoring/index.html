A little bit about us ...

Compass Tutoring is an online tutoring platform for students in grades 1 to 10. In a tutoring session, students will be provided with quality education and all the best academic resources. We provide students with academic support and tutors with opportunities. We aim to make quality tutoring accessible around the country for very reasonable and affordable costs.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When do I have to pay for my sessions?

    The first lesson is free. For the following lessons, you will have to pay for your package as soon as you purchase it. If you don't pay prior to the student's second tutoring lesson, the tutoring session will be postponed until you pay. It is strongly encouraged that you pay within the next 24 hours after the purchase of the package.

  • What happens if the student cannot make it to the tutoring lesson?

    Please contact your tutor via email if the student isn't able to attend the lesson. Together, we will reschedule your lesson for a different time or day.

  • What do I do when my 8 weeks of tutoring sessions in over?

    When your 8 weeks ends, you can purchase another tutoring package (same or different) and register for another month of tutoring sessions. There is not limit to the number of times you can purchase tutoring sessions.

  • What do I do if I have concerns about the student's tutor?

    Although this is very unlikely, in this case you would have two options. You can either send us a feedback form, which will be attached to the email that you would have received from your tutor prior to your first sessions, or, you can email our organisation and we will personally address your concerns.

  • How do I pay for the tutoring package if I don't have any of the paying resources listed above?

    You can email us and we will work together on an alternative that suits you the best. In addition, to PayPal and money transfer via email, we can also accept cheques.

  • Can I request a specific tutor?

    We cannot guarantee that the student will be paired with a specific tutor, but our team will do their best to follow your preferences.
